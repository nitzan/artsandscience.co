Soft Offering: Foreword

“Although it should be read as science fiction, current trends in miniaturization and price reduction almost guarantee that many of notions discussed will actually happen in the near future”

Alan C. Kay–Xerox Palo Alto Research Center–1972

The nature of this text is philosophical. At times Utopian. The point of the thinking is to provoke a different approach to designing technology. More responsible, sustainable and conducive to our future.

Moving focus from abilities, to intent, from efficiency to merit.
